Warranty

At Mach Five Wheels, we stand behind the quality of our forged wheels. All coverage is subject to the terms and limitations below.

1. Structural Warranty

We provide a limited lifetime warranty against structural defects in materials and workmanship for forged wheels purchased through us or an authorized dealer. This warranty applies only under normal, intended driving conditions and is our sole obligation for structural issues.

2. Finish Warranty

Each wheel finish carries a one-year warranty from the date of delivery against clear-coat peeling, powder-coat separation, and excessive discoloration under normal care. Improper cleaning or use of harsh chemicals, abrasives, or non-pH-neutral products is expressly excluded from this coverage.

3. Warranty Conditions

To qualify for warranty service, the wheels must:

  • Remain unaltered and fitted on the originally intended vehicle
  • Be installed and torqued by certified professionals
  • Maintained with proper cleaning and care (pH-neutral products only)

4. Exclusions

This warranty does not cover damage caused by:

  • Improper installation or incorrect torque
  • Off-road use, racing, or track abuse (case specific exemptions may apply)
  • Chemical exposure (acidic cleaners, brake dust corrosion)
  • Impact with curbs, potholes, or road debris
  • Improper mounting or dismounting of tires
