Design Without Asterisks

We don’t “adapt” a wheel for release, we engineer it to perform at the highest level. Every new pattern is modeled, stress-tested, and machined from specialized forgings with thermal behavior and fatigue resistance in mind, ensuring it excels across a range of applications.

The upcoming releases continue this approach: zero compromises, and more clarity in how a wheel should behave at speed.
